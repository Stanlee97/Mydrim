WALLACE EJOR

Contemporary Visual Artist | b. 1958

Contemporary Visual Artist

Wallace Ejor is one of Nigeria's accomplished visual artists known for his distinctive style and creative expression. His work explores themes of contemporary African society, personal identity, and universal human experiences through a compelling visual language.

With a masterful command of color, form, and composition, Ejor creates works that resonate with audiences across cultural boundaries. His paintings, predominantly executed in oil on canvas, demonstrate a sophisticated understanding of traditional artistic techniques combined with innovative contemporary approaches.

Ejor's contributions to Nigerian contemporary art have earned him recognition both nationally and internationally, establishing him as a significant voice in the discourse of modern African artistic practice.
