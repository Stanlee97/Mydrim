FIDELIS ODOGWU

Master Sculptor | b. 1970

Master Sculptor

Fidelis Odogwu is one of Nigeria's Master Sculptors, renowned for his innovative approach to three-dimensional form and artistic expression. With a career dedicated to exploring the boundaries of sculptural practice, Odogwu has created works that challenge perceptions and inspire contemplation.

His sculptures, characterized by a sophisticated interplay of volume, space, and materiality, reflect a deep understanding of contemporary aesthetic principles combined with cultural sensitivity. Odogwu's work has been featured in significant exhibitions and collections, establishing him as a prominent figure in contemporary African sculpture.
