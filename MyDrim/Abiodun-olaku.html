ABIODUN OLAKU

Master Painter | b. 1958

Master Painter & Visual Storyteller

Abiodun Olaku is one of Nigeria's Master Artists, a most revered Painter and visual storyteller. With a career spanning decades, Olaku has established himself as a pioneering figure in contemporary African art. His works are characterized by a profound exploration of cultural identity, human emotion, and the complexities of modern African society.

Through his distinctive style and masterful use of color and form, Abiodun Olaku continues to captivate audiences and contribute significantly to the global conversation on African artistic expression. His paintings are held in prestigious collections and have been exhibited internationally.
