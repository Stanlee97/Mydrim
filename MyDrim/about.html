The Mydrim Story

Modern and Contemporary African Art

Mydrim Gallery
was established in Lagos
Nigeria in 1992

A reality borne out of a dream rooted in a passion for the beauty expressed through art. The gallery, located at 74B Norman Williams Street, S.W Ikoyi, just off the busy Awolowo Road, seeks to focus on the wealth, beauty, and message of African art in general and Nigerian art in particular.
