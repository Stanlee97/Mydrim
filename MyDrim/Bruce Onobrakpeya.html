BRUCE ONOBRAKPEYA PhD, MFR

Master Artist, Printmaker & Educator | b. 1932

Legendary Master Artist

Bruce Onobrakpeya is a legendary Nigerian Master Artist, Printmaker, Painter and Educator whose artistic contributions have shaped modern art across Africa. As a PhD holder and Member of the Federal Republic (MFR), Prof. Onobrakpeya has profoundly influenced generations of artists and art scholars.

With a prolific career spanning over six decades, his works in printmaking, painting, and sculpture demonstrate an innovative fusion of traditional African artistic practices with contemporary techniques. His explorations of cultural identity, spirituality, and human experience have earned him international acclaim and numerous prestigious awards.

Onobrakpeya's pedagogical contributions through teaching have established him as a mentor to many prominent contemporary African artists, making him an indispensable figure in African art history.
